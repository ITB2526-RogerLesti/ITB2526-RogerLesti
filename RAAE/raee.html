3. WEEE Management Protocol (RAEE)

Legal Framework: Royal Decree 110/2015 | Directive 2012/19/EU
01

Selective Collection

Never mix cables, circuit boards, or screens with regular waste. Use dedicated containers based on material (Plastic/Metal vs. Electronics).

02

Safe Storage

Lithium batteries must be kept in cool, dry places to prevent fires. Equipment should not be dismantled to avoid heavy metal leaks (lead/mercury).

03

Authorized Transport

The center must contact an Authorized Waste Manager to ensure legal processing and receive a formal recycling certificate.

Environmental Security Notice

Proper WEEE management prevents environmental contamination and promotes the circular economy within the ITB community.
