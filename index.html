♻️

WEEE Waste Management (RD 110/2015)

1

Selective Collection

Strict separation of cables, screens and circuit boards. Never in general waste.

2

Safe Storage

Dry storage areas for batteries. Avoid disassembly without proper protective equipment.

3

Certified Disposal

Hand over to authorised waste managers to ensure full legal traceability.

💡 Our Strategic Proposal

We propose the "Reuse Workshop": a student-managed repair lab where ITB hardware is diagnosed, fixed and returned to service. This directly applies the Right to Repair principle, extends asset lifecycles and measurably reduces the institution's carbon footprint.
