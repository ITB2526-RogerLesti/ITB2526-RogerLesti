Year 1 · 2026 · Phase: Efficiency

Smart Monitoring & LED Infrastructure

Deploy digital intelligence to eliminate invisible energy waste — the lowest-hanging fruit.

🔍

IoT Energy Audit

We will instrument every IT classroom, the server room and common areas with IoT sensors (temperature, occupancy, power draw). The live dashboard will surface phantom loads — equipment left on overnight or during breaks — and optimise HVAC scheduling to match real occupancy. Studies show this alone reduces baseline electricity by 4–8%.

⚡ Electricity −4%
−4%
electricity
💡

LED Lighting + Motion Sensors

Replace all remaining fluorescent tubes with high-efficiency LED fixtures (5000K, CRI ≥ 80) and install passive infrared (PIR) occupancy sensors. Lights automatically switch off 10 minutes after the last person leaves. This is the single largest standalone electricity reduction at 9%, with an ROI typically under 2 years on energy savings alone.

⚡ Electricity −9%
−9%
electricity
🖥️

Intelligent Server Management

Consolidate underutilised physical servers into virtual machines (VMware / Proxmox), reducing the number of physical hosts by an estimated 40%. Implement Wake-on-LAN + scheduled shutdown scripts so servers power down outside teaching hours. Combined with the IoT audit data, this yields a 4% reduction in electricity — with zero impact on teaching quality.

Year 2 · 2027 · Phase: Circularity

Reuse Workshop, Solar & Digitalisation

From linear "buy–use–discard" to circular "repair–reuse–renew". The biggest cultural shift.

☀️

Solar Panels — Expanded Self-Consumption

Expand the existing rooftop photovoltaic system from its current capacity to cover an additional 13% of annual electricity demand. The ITB rooftop has sufficient south-facing area for a second array of ~25 kWp. Surplus generation feeds back into the grid under Spain's self-consumption regulation (RD 244/2019), generating compensation credits.

Right to Repair Workshop

ASIX (Network Systems Administration) students operate a weekly repair lab where broken school hardware — laptops, monitors, keyboards — is diagnosed, repaired and returned to active use instead of being discarded. Each repaired device avoids 200–400 kg of embodied carbon from a replacement purchase. This reduces consumable paper (repair docs, labels, logistics) by 5% and creates a tangible, curriculum-integrated sustainability project.

Full Document Digitalisation

Eliminate all printed copies for exams, meeting minutes, permission slips and internal communications. Migrate to a digital-first workflow using ITB's existing Google Workspace: Google Forms for assessments, Drive for documents, Classroom for assignment distribution. Target: 40% reduction in paper consumption. Teachers receive training and digital exam templates.

Year 3 · 2028–2029 · Phase: Independence

Water Optimisation & Eco Products

Close the loop — optimise every litre of water and every millilitre of cleaning product.

🌧️

Rainwater Harvesting System

Install a rooftop catchment and underground storage tank (est. 8,000 L capacity) to collect and filter rainwater for irrigation of the school grounds and outdoor cleaning operations. Barcelona averages 600 mm/year of rainfall — sufficient to cover 12% of total water demand. The system includes a first-flush diverter and sediment filter for water quality compliance.

💧 Water −12%
−12%
water
🚿

High-Efficiency Aerators

Retrofit all 40+ taps across the building with 4 L/min aerating inserts (down from 8–12 L/min standard) and install dual-flush mechanisms (3/6 L) on all 20+ toilets. These are low-cost, high-impact interventions with payback periods under 6 months. Combined with the rainwater system, this achieves an 18% reduction in mains water consumption.

💧 Water −18%

🧴

Eco-Label Concentrated Cleaning Products

Replace all conventional cleaning products with EU Eco-Label certified concentrates (dilution ratio 1:30 to 1:50). This reduces the physical volume of product purchased by up to 40%, lowers packaging waste, eliminates hazardous substances and reduces transport emissions. Supplier: certified by AENOR under EN ISO 14024. No additional training cost — same cleaning protocols, just different product formulations.
