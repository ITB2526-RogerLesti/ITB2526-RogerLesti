WEEE Management (Legal Framework RD 110/2015)

1

Selective Collection

Never mix cables or screens with regular waste. Use specific bins.

2

Safe Storage

Cool/dry places for batteries. Do not dismantle units locally.

3

Certified Disposal

Handover to Authorized Managers for legal recycling certificates.
